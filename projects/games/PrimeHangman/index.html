    How to Play

    Welcome to Prime Number Hangman! Here's how to play:

    1. A random prime number will be chosen based on your selected difficulty.
    2. You have attempts based on difficulty: 5 for 5-digit, 6 for 6-digit, and 7 for 7-digit numbers.
    3. Enter your guess by typing digits in each box
    4. After checking, each digit will be color-coded:
      • Green - Correct digit in the correct position
      • Blue - Correct digit in the wrong position
      • Red - Incorrect digit
    5. You can request clues to help you guess the number.

    Good luck!
